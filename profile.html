Ronald Ssentongo Simply known as Ronnie Vybz ,
is a Dancer, Dance Choreographer, Content Creator, Creative
and he is a member of the award-winning dance troupe
Triplets Ghetto Kids that performed alongside
French Montana and Swae Lee
at the2017 BET Awards.
Before being famous, Ronnie and the 8 other members of
the Triplets Ghetto Kids were either orphaned or ghetto dwellers.
They went viral in 2014 with a dance video choreographed
to the Eddy Kenzo song Sitya Loss.
His crew Triplets Ghetto Kids
won the 2016 African Entertainment Award USA
for Best Dance Group, a 2016 BEFTTA UK award
for Best Dance Act, and was nominated for Most
Outstanding Dance Group at the 2016 International Reggae
and World Music Awards,
Best African Dancers At Afrimma USA, Best Dance choreography at HIPIPO AWARDS. etc
Family Life:
He is from a Family of 8 children living in Kampala, Uganda, East Africa.
Associated With:
He has have made a number of videos where he dance to songs by top artists like Wizkid , Chris Brown, Kizz Daniel , black sherrif , Wande coal Etc
